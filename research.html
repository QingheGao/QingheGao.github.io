My work focuses on pushing the boundaries of what we know. Here's a look at my research interests and selected publications.

Focus Areas

RESEARCH
INTERESTS.

Machine Learning & Deep Learning

Developing graph neural networks and self-supervised learning methods to predict molecular properties, environmental impacts, and polymer behavior directly from molecular structures.

Reinforcement Learning

Using reinforcement learning and transfer learning to automate chemical process design — from generating flowsheets to optimizing process synthesis across different simulation fidelities.

Process Design & Chemical Engineering

Bridging AI and chemical engineering to tackle real-world challenges like sustainable process synthesis, life cycle assessment, and multi-agent systems for complex engineering workflows.
